Virtual Reality Digital Creation: 3D Modeling & Systems Programming Portfolio

This portfolio showcases various creations I developed on the virtual reality platform Rec Room. Using the built-in creation tool, Maker Pen, I designed 3D models, developed circuit-based logic systems, and created Dorm Rooms featuring a variety of interactive mechanisms, growing a subscriber base to 7.7K+ subscribers through continuous iteration, experimentation, and logic-driven design.

Project Classifications

My portfolio primarily centers on Dorm Room Design—the default entry environment for users on the Rec Room platform. In this space, I specialize in crafting builds that seamlessly balance aesthetic appeal, spatial efficiency, and engaging interactive mechanics. Beyond these compact spaces, I also challenge myself with large-scale Room Creations, building expansive environments designed for multi-user experiences to constantly push the boundaries of my design capabilities.

Creation Journey

I joined Rec Room as a player in 2021, but it didn't take long to discover the joy of unconstrained creation. I have always loved dreaming up eccentric, innovative concepts, and this platform suddenly provided the perfect canvas to bring my imagination to life in VR. It offered an ideal environment with a low barrier to entry but a high technical ceiling for advanced output. This sparked a three-year journey of continuous learning and creation, primarily dedicated to my Smart Dorm series, alongside various mini-projects and full-scale rooms.

With Rec Room scheduled to close on June 1, 2026, and because these proprietary, in-engine creations cannot be natively exported, I built this portfolio website to permanently archive and document my work.

Design Philosophy

My approach centers on maximizing creativity within limited space and resources by leveraging clever constraints and the right tools. While many creators strive for hyper-realism and intricate details, I lean toward a minimalist aesthetic infused with hidden mechanics and innovative, engaging interactions—unlocking the full potential of imagination within a space.

Smart Dorm — Intelligent Interactive Dormitory Series

Exploring highly functional automated geometric transformations within severely constrained spatial budgets.

Smart Dorm: The Starting Point of My Creator Journey in Rec Room

Earned: 1,571,500 Purchases: 2,245

As my first commercial release in Rec Room, this project explores spatial optimization and interactive design. I engineered the dorm with dynamic, space-saving mechanisms: a button-activated transforming bed, a wall-deployed TV, and retractable hidden stairs.

Officially featured by Rec Room, the project achieved thousands of sales in its first week. This explosive debut validated my design approach and laid the foundation for my signature Smart Dorm series.

Smart Dorm 2: Minimalist Aesthetic & Sleek Mechanics

Earned: 7,840,000 Purchases: 5,600

Leveraging my experience from the first project, I designed a second generation featuring a minimalist, modern aesthetic and a more seamless user experience. I introduced innovative mechanics to deliver a unique spatial experience, such as color-changing windows and an immersive tunnel-hovercar transition for player departures. This project ultimately became my best-selling and most successful creation to date.

Smart Dorm 3: Exploring Expansive Spatial Design

Earned: 4,683,350 Purchases: 5,589

Inspired by the futuristic luxury mansion in the movie Incredibles 2, this project was my first venture into designing expansive visual spaces. The highlight of this dorm is a custom-engineered indoor waterfall, seamlessly integrated alongside a new suite of smart furniture and advanced interactive mechanisms.

sDorm: Launching a Next-Generation Smart Dorm

Earned: 1,567,650 Purchases: 900

Serving as a masterclass of my technical expertise, this project integrates all my proven methods—including transformable interiors—into a brand-new series. The technical breakthrough of this build is a fully functional bus system, engineered through complex logic circuits and advanced systems design.

Smart Dorm 4: Elevated Aesthetics & Immersive Systems

Earned: 311,000 Purchases: 450

For this iteration, I merged advanced mechanics with a more sophisticated, stylish aesthetic to elevate the visual design. The core highlights of this project introduce engaging gameplay and systemic automation: a precision-engineered paper-tossing mini-game and a futuristic food-delivery drone system.

MasterBartender: Independent Room Creation

Global Visitors: 1,400+

As my premier independent room creation, MasterBartender showcases a fully realized arcade-style game inspired by the retro classic Root Beer Tapper. To push past platform constraints, I expanded the technical possibilities of the Maker Pen by integrating custom assets built in Unity. This workflow allowed me to implement advanced visual elements, including tailored skyboxes, flowing water streams, custom 3D models, and 2D UI graphics, all running on a backbone of complex logic circuits.

My development workflow leverages the Rec Room Maker Pen as a full-stack creation tool. I execute the entire pipeline independently—from asset modeling and spatial layout to programming advanced visual scripts, designing behavior trees, and architecting complex interactive systems.
