Virtual Reality Digital Creation: 3D Modeling & Programming Portfolio

This portfolio showcases multiple creations I developed on the virtual reality platform Rec Room. Using the built-in creation tool, Maker Pen, I designed 3D models, developed circuit-based logic systems, and created Dorm Rooms featuring a variety of interactive mechanisms, growing a subscriber base to 7.7K+ subscribers through continuous iteration, experimentation, and logic-driven design.

Project Types

My portfolio primarily centers on Dorm Room Design—the default entry environment for users on the Rec Room platform. In this space, I specialize in crafting builds that balance aesthetics, spatial efficiency, and interactive mechanics. Beyond these compact spaces, I also challenge myself with large-scale Room Creations, building expansive environments designed for multi-user experiences to constantly push the boundaries of my design capabilities.

Creation Journey

I joined Rec Room as a player in 2021, but it didn't take long to discover the joy of unconstrained creation. I have always loved dreaming up eccentric, innovative concepts, and this platform provided the perfect space to bring my imagination and creativity to life in VR. It offered an ideal environment with a low barrier to entry but a high technical ceiling for advanced output. This sparked a three-year journey of continuous learning and creation, primarily dedicated to my Smart Dorm series, alongside various mini-projects and full-scale rooms.

With Rec Room closing on June 1, 2026, and because these format-specific creations cannot be fully exported, I built this portfolio to document my work as thoroughly as possible.

Design Philosophy

My core approach is maximizing creativity within limited space and resources through clever thinking and the right tools. While many creators pursue hyper-realism and intricate details, I lean toward a minimalist aesthetic infused with hidden mechanics and innovative, engaging interactions—unlocking the full potential of imagination within a space.

Smart Dorm — Smart Dormitory Series

Exploring highly functional spatial design within severely constrained spaces.

Smart Dorm: The Starting Point of My Creator Journey in Rec Room

Earned: 1,571,500 Purchases: 2,245

As my first commercial release in Rec Room, this project explores spatial optimization and interactive design. I engineered the Dorm Room with dynamic space-saving mechanisms: a button-activated transforming bed, a wall-deployed TV, and retractable stairs.

Officially featured by Rec Room, the project achieved over a thousand sales in its first week. This explosive debut validated my design approach and laid the foundation for my signature Smart Dorm series.

Smart Dorm 2: Minimalist Aesthetic & Smoother Mechanics

Earned: 7,840,000 Purchases: 5,600

Leveraging my experience from the first project, I designed a second generation featuring a minimalist, modern aesthetic and a smoother user experience. I introduced innovative approaches to create a more unique experience, such as color-changing windows and an immersive tunnel-hovercar transition effect. This project ultimately became my best-selling and most successful creation to date.

Smart Dorm 3: Exploring Expansive Visual Spaces

Earned: 4,683,350 Purchases: 5,589

Inspired by the futuristic luxury mansion in the movie Incredibles 2, this project was my first venture into designing expansive visual spaces. The highlight of this dorm is a hidden indoor waterfall and water flow, integrated alongside a new suite of smart furniture.

sDorm: Next-Generation Smart Dorm

Earned: 1,567,650 Purchases: 900

Combining the various techniques I've explored, this project integrates all my proven methods—including transformable interiors—into a brand-new series. The technical breakthrough of this build is a fully functional bus system, engineered through complex logic circuits and systems design.

Smart Dorm 4: The Final Dance — Peak Aesthetics & Technology

Earned: 311,000 Purchases: 450

In this final creation, I employed more advanced and streamlined circuit programming, merged with a refined, modern aesthetic. The core highlights of this project: a precision-engineered ball-tossing mini-game and a futuristic drone delivery system.

My development method uses the Rec Room Maker Pen as a full-stack creation tool, independently executing the entire pipeline—from asset modeling and spatial layout to programming advanced visual scripts, designing behavior trees, and architecting complex interactive systems.
